# Service Architecture & Offerings {Pishi Studio}

Pishi Studio operates on a structured, phased methodology. By separating diagnosis from execution, we ensure strategic coherence and eliminate scope creep. All engagements follow the architectural sequence below.

## 1. Digital Authority Diagnostic™
*   **Purpose**: The foundational strategic audit.
*   **The Work**: A comprehensive forensic analysis of the client’s current digital infrastructure. We map the disconnects between brand positioning, website architecture, conversion logic, and operational performance.
*   **The Deliverable**: A formal "Structural Roadmap"—a transformation plan that defines the exact requirements for a cohesive digital system.
*   **Investment**: €3,500 – €6,000.
*   **Note**: This is the mandatory entry point for all new architecture engagements. No design or development work is performed during this phase.

## 2. Digital Authority Architecture™
*   **Purpose**: The system build and transformation phase.
*   **The Work**: Designing the integrated digital ecosystem. This involves re-engineering website architecture, establishing high-precision visual systems, building copywriting frameworks, and calibrating content visibility.
*   **The Deliverable**: A fully designed, high-performance, and cohesive front-end digital presence.
*   **Investment**: €10,000 – €15,000.
*   **Note**: Execution only commences upon the completion of the *Digital Authority Diagnostic™* roadmap.

## 3. Growth Infrastructure Layer
*   **Purpose**: Operational scalability and system governance.
*   **The Work**: Integrating the back-end architecture with front-end presence. This includes designing clean CRM structures, automating lead qualification and follow-up flows, and establishing data governance.
*   **The Deliverable**: A transparent, automated, and scalable operational backbone that allows the digital presence to function without manual intervention.
*   **Investment**: €3,000 – €6,000.
*   **Note**: Often integrated as a parallel stream during *Digital Authority Architecture™* or as a standalone optimization for existing systems.

---

## Architectural Rules for Engagement

1.  **Fixed-Scope, Fixed-Fee**: Every service is priced based on the architectural scope defined in the *Diagnostic™* phase. We do not engage in hourly billing or undefined "maintenance retainers."
2.  **No Arbitrary Modification**: The services exist as modular layers of a larger system. We do not "mix and match" services in a way that compromises the architectural integrity of the final output.
3.  **Governance**: All work is governed by the *Strategic Core*—ensuring that every design choice supports the broader business objective of authority and clarity.
